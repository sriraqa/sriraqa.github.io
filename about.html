A bit about me 😊


I am a Systems Design Engineering student at The University of Waterloo aspiring to become a product designer. In the past, I have attended hackathons and competitions like Hack the North and Technovation. I have also worked at Kumon as a Teaching Assistant.


My interest in product design came naturally to me since I have always loved creating art and learning about technology. Also, topics in psychology and philosophy like colour theory and ethics are exciting for me to learn about and discuss.
